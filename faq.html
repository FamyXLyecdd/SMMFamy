Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is SMMFamy? ↓

SMMFamy is a Social Media Marketing (SMM) panel that provides affordable services to help grow your social media presence. We offer followers, likes, views, comments, and more for popular platforms like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, and many others.

Is it safe to use SMMFamy? ↓

Yes! Our services are 100% safe to use. We use proven methods that comply with social media platform guidelines. We never ask for your passwords - you only need to provide your profile or post link.

How quickly will I see results? ↓

Most orders start processing within minutes and complete within 24-72 hours depending on the service and quantity. Some services are instant delivery while others use gradual/drip-feed delivery for a more natural look.

Do you offer refunds? ↓

Yes, we offer refunds in the following cases:

  • Order not processed within 48 hours
  • Wrong link submitted (before order starts)
  • Service unavailable after payment

Please note that once an order is completed or partially completed, refunds may only be partial based on the remaining quantity.

Payment Questions

What payment methods do you accept? ↓

We currently accept the following payment methods:

  • GCash - The most popular e-wallet in the Philippines
  • Maya (PayMaya) - Another widely used e-wallet

We're working on adding more payment options including bank transfers and credit cards.

How do I add funds to my account? ↓

To add funds:

  • Go to the "Add Funds" page
  • Enter the amount you want to add (minimum ₱50)
  • Select your payment method (GCash or Maya)
  • Send the exact amount to the displayed number
  • Take a screenshot of your payment
  • Click "I've Paid" and contact us on Messenger with the screenshot

Your funds will be credited within 5-30 minutes after verification.

What is the minimum deposit amount? ↓

The minimum deposit amount is ₱50. There is no maximum limit, but for large deposits (over ₱10,000), please contact us first.

How long does it take to credit my payment? ↓

After you send proof of payment via Messenger, your funds will typically be credited within:

  • 5-15 minutes during business hours (9 AM - 10 PM)
  • 30-60 minutes outside business hours

Please be patient and avoid sending multiple messages. We process payments as quickly as possible!

Service Questions

What is the difference between different service types? ↓

We offer several types of services:

  • Real/Organic - Real accounts from active users (highest quality)
  • High Quality (HQ) - Good quality accounts with profile pictures
  • Mixed - Combination of real and bot accounts (best value)
  • Bot - Automated accounts (cheapest, lowest quality)
  • With Refill - Free replacement if followers drop

Choose based on your budget and quality requirements.

What does "Refill" mean? ↓

Refill means we will replace any followers/likes that drop within a specified period (usually 30 days). If you notice a significant drop, you can request a free refill through the Orders page. This ensures you always get what you paid for!

What is Drip Feed? ↓

Drip Feed is a feature that gradually delivers your order over a period of time instead of all at once. This makes the growth look more natural and is less likely to trigger spam filters. For example, instead of getting 1000 followers in one hour, you can spread it over 24 hours.

My order is stuck on "Pending". What should I do? ↓

If your order has been pending for more than 24 hours:

  • Check if your profile/post is public (private accounts won't work)
  • Verify the link is correct and accessible
  • Contact our support via the Tickets page

We'll investigate and either complete the order or issue a refund.

Can I cancel my order? ↓

Orders can only be canceled if they haven't started processing yet. Once an order starts, it cannot be canceled. If you need to cancel a pending order, please contact support immediately through the Tickets page.

Account Questions

Do I need my Instagram/TikTok password? ↓

No! We NEVER ask for your passwords. We only need the link to your profile or post. Anyone asking for your password is a scammer. Our services work without requiring access to your account.

Will my account get banned? ↓

Our services are designed to be safe and undetectable. We use gradual delivery and real-looking accounts to minimize any risk. However, to be extra safe:

  • Don't order huge quantities all at once
  • Use drip-feed for larger orders
  • Maintain natural activity on your account
  • Don't change your username while orders are processing
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ↓

Currently, you can reset your password by contacting support via Messenger. We're working on an automated password reset feature. Please have your email and recent order details ready for verification.
